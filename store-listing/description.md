# MB Tuner

A focused chromatic tuner for musicians. Clean, fast, free — no ads, no
accounts, no tracking.

## Features

- Chromatic tuning from E1 to E7 with cent-accurate readouts
- Smooth, low-latency pitch detection
- A reference selectable from 440–445 Hz
- Elegant strobe-ring visualization
- Beautiful light and dark themes that follow your system
- Subtle haptic feedback when you lock in tune
- Full VoiceOver and Dynamic Type support

## Privacy

MB Tuner analyzes audio from your microphone **entirely on-device**. Nothing
is recorded, stored, or sent anywhere. No analytics. No ads. No accounts.
